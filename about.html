About Marry.mk

Your trusted partner in planning the perfect wedding

Our Story

Marry.mk was founded with a simple mission: to make wedding planning easier and more enjoyable for couples across North Macedonia. We understand that planning a wedding can be overwhelming, which is why we've created a comprehensive directory that brings together the best wedding venues, services, and vendors in one place.

Our platform features carefully curated listings from all major cities including Skopje, Tetovo, Gostivar, Ohrid, Bitola, and more. Whether you're looking for an elegant wedding hall, a talented photographer, or a skilled makeup artist, Marry.mk is your one-stop destination.
